About Section

Welcome to Goat Burger, where passion meets flavour in every bite. Nested in the heart of Dublin's city centre, our restaraunt is a haven for burger enthusiasts seeking for all out excellence. We take pride in providing an experience for our customers, and that's why we only source the finest quality ingredients, from locally sourced grass fed beef, to artisanal cheeses and freshly baked brioche buns.

Quality Prices

Our burgers are not only renowned for their exceptional quality, but also for being affordaly priced. Despite the premium ingredients and meticulous craftsmanship that go into each burger, we strive to ensure that our menu remains accessible to all customers.
